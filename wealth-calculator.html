Wealth Calculator

Estimate your retirement savings based on your current age, income, desired retirement age, and expected investment return. This calculator assumes contributions to a Self-Managed Super Fund (SMSF) and is aligned with Australian Taxation Office (ATO) preservation age rules (age 60).

Note: This calculator provides an estimate based on a compound interest formula and assumes 15% concessional contributions from your income, as per ATO guidelines.
