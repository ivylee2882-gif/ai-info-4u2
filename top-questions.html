Top Questions About SMSFs

Who Can Set Up an SMSF?

  • Who is eligible? Australian residents over 18 who are not disqualified under the SIS Act. ATO Eligibility
  • Can minors be members? Yes, with a parent or guardian as trustee. SMSF Association
  • Maximum members? Up to six members, typically family or business associates. ATO Setup
  • Can non-residents join? Yes, if residency conditions are met. ATO Residency
  • Disqualified persons? Bankrupts or those convicted of dishonesty offenses cannot be trustees. SMSF Association

  • Cost-effectiveness? May be cheaper for high balances. SMSF Association

How to Set Up an SMSF?

  • First step? Appoint trustees or a corporate trustee. ATO Setup
  • Trust deed? Create a compliant trust deed. SMSF Services
  • Register with ATO? Obtain an ABN and register. ATO Setup
  • Bank account? Set up a dedicated SMSF account. ATO Bank Account
  • Investment strategy? Develop and document a strategy. SMSF Association

How to Manage an SMSF?

  • Annual audit? Arrange an independent audit yearly. ATO Audit
  • Record keeping? Maintain accurate records for 5+ years. ATO Records
  • Compliance? Adhere to SIS Act and ATO rules. ATO Compliance
  • Asset valuation? Value assets annually at market rates. ATO Valuation
  • Tax obligations? Lodge annual returns with ATO. SMSF Association
