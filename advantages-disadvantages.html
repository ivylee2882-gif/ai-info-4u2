Advantages & Disadvantages of SMSFs

Advantages

  • Investment Control: Trustees can tailor investments to personal goals. ATO Benefits
  • Tax Efficiency: Concessional tax rates on earnings and contributions. SMSF Association
  • Flexibility: Ability to invest in diverse assets like property or shares. ATO Investing
  • Estate Planning: Direct control over beneficiary nominations. ATO Benefits
  • Cost Savings: Potentially lower fees for high balances compared to retail funds. SMSF Services

Disadvantages

  • Compliance Burden: Strict ATO regulations require diligent record-keeping. ATO Compliance
  • Time-Intensive: Managing investments and audits demands significant time. SMSF Association
  • High Costs: Setup and ongoing costs can be expensive for low balances. SMSF Services
  • Risk of Penalties: Breaches can lead to fines or disqualification. SMSF Association
  • Limited Diversification: Smaller funds may struggle to diversify investments. ATO Investing
